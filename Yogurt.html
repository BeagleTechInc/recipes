Local Veggie Curry in Coconut Broth

Yield: 2 Big Servings or 4 Light Servings
Author: Leanne Valenti
Prep time: 30 Min Cook time: 20 Min Total time: 50 Min
An ideal weeknight dinner, this nourishing curry recipe is sure to delight your friends and family while supporting a great cause.

Ingredients

  • 800g prepared veggies of your choice (Pro Tip: Start with about 2lb of raw veggies)
  • 1–2 Tbsp avocado oil
  • 1-inch ginger root, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp SE Asian Seasoning Mix
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 quart cooked jasmine rice (1 cup per person)
  • Fresh cilantro, to garnish

Instructions

Cook the Veggies
  1. Prepare each vegetable as desired (boil, steam, or grill) to retain their distinct textures and flavors. See suggestions for how to prepare different vegetables above.
Prepare the Broth
  1. Heat avocado oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add minced ginger and garlic, sautéing until fragrant (2–3 minutes).
  3. Stir in SE Asian Seasoning Mix, coconut milk, and water.
  4. Simmer gently for 5–10 minutes.
Finish & Serve
  1. Gently fold in the cooked veggies, simmering together for another 1–2 minutes to ensure everything is coated in the creamy broth.
  2. Adjust salt to taste.
  3. To serve, spoon the curry over jasmine rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.
